Biography

I am currently a senior member of the technical staff at Sandia National Laboratories. I earned a Ph.D. in Statistics from The Ohio State University in 2014 where I developed ‘Restricted Likelihood Methods’ to condition on robust summary statistics in Bayesian models. Current work focuses on a number of areas including analysis of complex computer models, functional data analysis, space-time models, inverse problems. My CV
